# ADR-0002: Define Nexa as the Codex Nexus Identity

## Status

Accepted

## Date

2026-07-17

---

## Context

Codex Nexus requires a recognizable identity representing the relationship between humans, knowledge, and technology.

The platform concept is based on:

- Connecting information
- Discovering relationships between knowledge sources
- Creating an intelligent knowledge environment

A symbolic identity helps communicate the purpose and philosophy of the project.

---

## Decision

The name **Nexa** has been chosen as the digital knowledge companion and symbolic identity of Codex Nexus.

Nexa represents:

- Connection
- Exploration
- Intelligence
- Navigation through knowledge

Nexa is designed to assist users in understanding, organizing, and discovering their own information.

Nexa does not replace human judgment; it empowers users by revealing meaningful connections within their knowledge.

---

## Rationale

A short and memorable identity makes the platform easier to recognize and strengthens its brand.

The name naturally derives from **Nexus**, reinforcing the idea that knowledge becomes more valuable when it is organized and connected.

Nexa reflects the core philosophy of Codex Nexus:

> *"Where knowledge comes together."*

---

## Consequences

### Positive

- Strong and memorable project identity
- Clear representation of the knowledge connection concept
- Consistent branding across the platform
- Foundation for future AI-assisted features and user experience

### Negative

- Additional design considerations for future interfaces, documentation, and branding assets
- The identity should be used consistently to avoid confusion

---

## Alternatives Considered

The following names and concepts were considered during the design phase:

- Nexus Entity
- Nexi
- Cael
- Other symbolic companion concepts

Nexa was selected because it best balanced simplicity, originality, memorability, and alignment with the vision of Codex Nexus.
